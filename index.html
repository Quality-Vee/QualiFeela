About UPP

Underground Production Promoter (UPP) began as a passion project to give a voice to the artists on the fringe. We believe in music that breaks boundaries and challenges the norm. From warehouse parties to intimate gigs, we find the beats that matter and bring them to you. Join our movement and support the future of music.
